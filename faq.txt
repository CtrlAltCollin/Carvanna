Welcome to the Carvana FAQ! Below you can find commonly asked questions by customers, and their answers.

Q: How does the Carvana buying process work?
A: The emtire process is done online. Customers brows the catalog, view detailed vehicle information, choose a car, and then select either delivery or physical pickup.
After purchase, customers receive a 7-day money-back trial period to decide whether they want to keep the car.

Q: Can I test drive the car before buying it?
A: Carvana does not offer test drives before purchase. However, every purchase includes a 7-day money-back gurantee, alowing you to drive the car for a week and return if you're not satisfied.

Q: Are prices negotiable?
A: No. Carvana uses fixed, non-negotiable pricing with no haggling and no hidden fees.

Q: What financing options are available?
A: Carvana offers financing options directly through their website. Customers can view estimated monthly payments during the checkout process.

Q: Can I use third-party financing?
A: Yes. Customers may use outside lenders for financing if they prefer.

Q: How do I know the car is in good function?
A: Carvana vehicles undergo a 150-point inspection, include high-quality photographs, and are backed by a 100-day/4,189-mile limited warranty through SilverRock.

Q: Does Carvana deliver cars?
A: Yes. Delivery is available depending on your area. Delivery fees apply.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Delivery times vary based on location, inventory, and scheduling. Customers will receive updates through email or text.

Q: Can I choose physical pickup instead od delivery?
A: Yes. Customers may pick up their vehicle from a Carvana location if they prefer.

Q: Does Carvana accept trade-ins?
A: Yes. You can trade in your current vehicle during checkout. Carvana allows customers to sell their car even if they are not purchasing another one.

Q: What happens to my trade-in if i return the car I purchased?
A: If you return the purchased vehcile during the 7-day money-back period, your trade-in will be returned to you.

Q: Does Carvana handle the title and registration process?
A: Yes. Carvana typically handles registration, title transfer, and necessary paperwork on behalf of the customer.

Q: Is there a warranty?
A: Yes. Most vehicles include a 100-day/4,189-mile limited warranty, and customers may purchase extended coverage through SilverRock.

Q: Are there any hidden fees?
A: No. Carvana's pricing is transparent. Taxes, registration fees, and (if applicable) delivery fees are shown during checkout.
